With rapid urbanization pacing at the speed of lightning, reduction in greenery has also become rapid resulting in higher temperatures and increased dust pollution. To address this issue, people started to plant greens like fruits, vegetables, plants, herbs, etc. in their homes, or say above them.

                
The terrace garden is one of many ways to lead a healthier and greener life. And it is a great opportunity for people with bigger spaces. Not only small plants or flowers, but it is a great idea to grow fruits and vegetables on your rooftop as well.

            

        



        

            

                
The best part of terrace gardening is that it can be completely covered in soil and grass to convert it into a lawn. After all, there can never be enough creativity and innovation done when it comes to gardening landscapes.

                
Now, it is not difficult as you think it is. And if you are a first-timer, all you need are a few things to keep in mind before you start planting your favorite greens. Things like knowing the space, types of plants that will fit the best, the amount of sunlight exposure, if the proper shade is required in harsh and direct sunlight areas, and the list goes on.
